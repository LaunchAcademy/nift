Nift was created to showcase the most innovative, most exciting new and future products from startup companies. Some of them might not be ready for prime-time yet but are on their way. You might not recognize many of them, but you will soon!

Browse the homepage to see the soon-to-be hottest gadgets. Think it's the next 'it' thing? Give it a high rating! Think it will flop? Tell us why!

Help out your fellow prophesiers and don't forget to upvote the thoughtful reviews, and downvote those annoying trolls.
